## VERIFIED transport facts (deep-research, this session — good for July 2026)

- **YYC ↔ Canmore drive:** ~1h20.
- **Banff Airporter** → Canmore: **$81.90 CAD one-way** (~$60 USD). Earliest Canmore→YYC departure **5:40am → arrives YYC ~7:00am**. Books online; reserve for peak July.
- **Pursuit / Brewster Banff Airport Express** → Canmore: from **~$66.75 CAD one-way**. Earliest Canmore→YYC **6:55am → arrives ~8:30am**.
- **Teleport private van:** **$350 CAD flat** one-way to Canmore, up to 8 pax. (Backup only — group-drive replaces it.)
- **Roam Transit:** in-valley only (Canmore–Banff–Lake Louise), **$6/ride**. NOT an airport service. Good for in-town/Banff day movement.
- **On-It regional bus:** **SUSPENDED summer 2026** (Roam notice Apr 8 2026). DO NOT plan on it.
- **YYC rental counters:** NO 24-hour counters. Most close 11:59pm. **Avis/Budget open latest, to ~1:00am.**
- **Moraine Lake:** private cars BANNED — shuttle/transit only (book Parks Canada shuttle). **Lake Minnewanka:** free shuttle.
