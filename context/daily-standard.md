## Done Threshold

**2 of 3 required targets hit = acceptable day.** Move on without guilt.
**All targets hit = complete day.** Note it — consistency compounds.
**0 of 3 = missed day.** Don't make it 2 in a row.

The goal is not perfection. It's a rate: hit your targets most days and the goals take care of themselves.
