**Trade-offs considered:** Loses the embodied/skill quality boxing was supposed to deliver as a BJJ substitute. Accepted because (a) that quality returns Jul 12+ when BJJ + Muay Thai ramp on original cadence (~6 weeks away), (b) the cut-phase keystone only needs to hold the recurring-slot + own-gravity benefit, which a Sunday morning treadmill block delivers, and (c) "she sees you reliably absent for 60–90 min every Sunday morning" still satisfies the desire-polarity "small space without explaining" pattern, just without the distinctive-skill-class layer. Less unique-to-Will than a combat class, but the cost/benefit on the cut wins decisively.
