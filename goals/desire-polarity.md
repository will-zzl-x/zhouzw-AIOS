> **Why this exists instead of a dread/RedPill plan:** The instinct that drew Will to Steele's guide — a staged ladder with focuses, timelines, and practical actions — is right. The *mechanism* (inducing a partner's fear of replacement to lever desire/compliance) is the inverse of differentiation and corrosive to a healthy relationship. This doc delivers the structure without the manipulation. The line, held throughout: **change yourself and let desire follow — never manage Elena's fear to extract it.**

## G — Goal

**What:** Shift the dynamic from desire-as-accommodation (Elena initiating from love-fuel / wanting to make Will happy) toward desire-as-want (hunger for Will specifically). See `perel.md` L35–37 for the diagnosed dynamic.

**Why:** This isn't a logistics problem to optimize — it's the long-term erotic health of the marriage Will is building. Done right, the same work compounds the rest of his life (career gravity, fitness, friendships, mission). The polarity is a *byproduct* of differentiation, not a target to hit.

**Anti-goals (hard lines — violating any of these means the plan failed):**
- **No dread, no games, no manufactured competition, no withholding affection as leverage.** That's the opposite of this doc.
- **Don't announce the plan to Elena.** Disrupting routine, taking space, building gravity — all done *unilaterally*, without explanation or justification (`perel.md` L52, L62).
- **No optimization/scoring talk — ever.** Elena explicitly asked for this to stop (`perel.md` L17, L45). The crucible is internal.
- **Don't process the dynamic *with* her warm.** Self-soothe and process via AIOS instead (`perel.md` L63).

## P — Plan: The Differentiation Ladder

Built as the staged progression Will wanted — but every rung is **self-directed**. You climb by becoming more yourself, not by acting on Elena. Phases overlap; they're a stack, not a sequence you finish and leave behind.

### Phase 0 — Stop the leaks *(immediate, ongoing)*
**Focus:** Remove what's actively suppressing desire before adding anything.
- Kill optimization/scoring talk and any mental grading of encounters (`perel.md` L60).
- Catch and stop over-pursuit — when you feel the pull to chase, redirect into your own thing (`perel.md` L61).
- Stop bringing the dynamic to her as a conversation (`perel.md` L44, L58).

**Checkpoint to advance:** One full week with zero optimization talk and at least one caught-and-redirected over-pursuit urge.

### Phase 1 — Build your own gravity *(weeks, ongoing — the core work)*
**Focus:** Have a self worth wanting, independent of the relationship. This is Schnarch's differentiation and Perel's "own gravity" (`perel.md` L39; `schnarch.md` L7–11).
- **Solo-pursuit night** — lock the recurring slot you keep slipping (currently 0/2 weeks). This is the literal mechanism of "an intellectual/social life Elena doesn't fully have access to."
- Keep career momentum visible, not narrated (SCM II work, acquisition project).
- NYC Cut is already this — physical discipline is gravity. Stay on it.
- Maintain your own friendships and outside interests as live, not theoretical.

**Checkpoint to advance:** Solo-pursuit night happening weekly for 2 straight weeks; you can name what in your week is *yours alone*.

### Phase 2 — Restore distance & choice *(layer in once Phase 1 holds)*
**Focus:** Reintroduce space so intimacy becomes *chosen*, not *default-available* (`perel.md` L11–15).
- Disrupt the merger-leaning routine **unilaterally** — vary the pattern (timing, default availability), don't announce it (`perel.md` L62).
- Keep small amounts of time apart without explanation (`perel.md` L52). Elena's two-week absence improved the dynamic — that's the mechanism, not a coincidence (`perel.md` L9, L35).
- **Selectivity:** when an initiation reads as accommodation rather than want, decline gently — don't reward the accommodation pattern (`perel.md` L50, L59).

**Checkpoint to advance:** Comfortable taking space and being selective without it feeling like a tactic or creating anxiety in *you*.

### Phase 3 — Presence & polarity in the moment *(advanced, ongoing)*
**Focus:** Self-validated intimacy under the crucible's pressure (`schnarch.md` L7–8).
- When intimacy happens, be fully there — no performance, no scoring (`perel.md` L51).
- Lead decisively; hold your own center in friction instead of accommodating to lower anxiety (`schnarch.md` L7, L11).

**Checkpoint:** This is the destination, not a box to tick — the work is continuous.

**Realism check:**
- In theory (will this work if followed?): high — it's straight from the council's diagnosis, and the distance-mechanism already showed up naturally during Elena's absence.
- In practice (will Will follow through?): the risk is **impatience**. Differentiation is slow on purpose — that slowness is exactly why dread is seductive (it promises fast results). If you find yourself wanting a faster lever, that urge is the signal to re-read this doc, not to switch frameworks.

- Any over-pursuit urges caught and redirected? (Phase 0)

**North-star signal (observed lightly, not scored):** desire-fuel (hunger for Will specifically) vs. love-fuel (wanting connection/accommodation) — `perel.md` L37. Notice it; don't measure it.

**Guardrail (Gottman/Evans):** If any move in here ever starts to feel like leverage, fear-induction, or contempt — stop. That's the dread line, and crossing it forfeits the trust the marriage runs on.
