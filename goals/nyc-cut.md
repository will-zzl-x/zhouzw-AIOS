## G — Goal

**What:** 172 lb / 12.5% BF by July 4, 2026. Starting from ~181 lb / ~19% BF. Net: ~9 lb loss, ~6.5% BF reduction, lean mass preserved.

**Why:** NYC trip July 30 — want to feel and look strong, not just lighter. Also: proving the cut works before the fall and building the habit of being in a controlled deficit. The intermediate strength standards don't care about bodyweight but body comp matters for how training feels and how the NYC trip feels.

**Anti-goals:**
- Don't lose muscle — lean mass target ≥145 lb throughout
- Don't let the cut consume mental energy or dominate social situations with Elena
- Don't sacrifice training performance chasing scale weight

---

## P — Plan

**Major moves:**
1. **Protein ≥ 150g/day** — muscle preservation lever. Non-negotiable. Hit this before anything else.
2. **Calories ≤ 2,200/day** — the deficit. Track on MyFitnessPal every day during cut. No tracking = no deficit = no results.
3. **GVS Ravage 3x/week minimum** — training stimulus preserves muscle during deficit. Miss a week and the deficit eats lean mass.
4. **10,000 steps/day** — passive caloric expenditure. Walking treadmill at desk, beach walks, general movement. Closes the deficit gap without extra gym time.
5. **Morning weigh-in daily** — feedback loop. Hard rule: 176+ = deficit week, don't ease up.

**Realism check:**
- In theory (will this plan work?): 90% — 9 lb in 7.5 weeks at 2,200 cal + training is math. It works.
- In practice (will I follow through?): 75% — weekends and social situations are the risk. MyFitnessPal adherence is the make-or-break habit.

**Action on the 75%:** Log food in MFP before eating when possible (front-load the decision). Weekend plan: same targets, allow 2,400 cal on one day if needed — not a cheat day, just a flex day.

**Crystal ball — top 3 failure reasons + mitigations:**

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|
| Calories blow over on weekends / social dinners — deficit disappears | One flex day/week (≤2,400 cal) built in. All other days strict 2,200. Log everything. |
| Protein falls short → deficit eats muscle → worse body comp at finish | Protein first at every meal. Hit 150g before filling remaining calories with anything else. |
| Training sessions skipped when work gets busy in cut phase | Sessions in calendar as non-negotiable. Same three slots each week. |

## S — System

**Tracking:**
- MyFitnessPal: daily calories + protein
- Morning scale: daily weigh-in, log in `/health-os`
- Weekly progress: `/health-os check` every Sunday — surface avg calories, protein hit rate, weight trend
- Training: `/workout-log` every session — track progressive overload, flag if strength is dropping (early signal of muscle loss)

**Reminders:** Nutrition targets are #2 and #3 on every daily check. Morning coffee surfaces them. Scale is the daily feedback loop.

**Accountability:** Weight trend visible weekly. If scale isn't moving by week 3 (May 17 + 21 days = ~June 7), audit the MFP log for calorie creep before adjusting targets.
