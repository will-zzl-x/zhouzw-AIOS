## Sunday 6/7

**~90 min active.**

4. **#25 Costco Chipotle Chicken Thigh Mexican Bowls — 5 srv**
   - Bake chipotle thighs (425°F ~30 min), slice into chunks
   - Cook 1.5 cups white rice (rice cooker or pot)
   - Sauté frozen pepper & onion mix with avocado oil + cumin + chili powder + salt (~10 min)
   - Warm + drain 1 can black beans
   - Portion 5 containers: rice + beans + peppers/onions + thigh chunks

   - ~60 min

5. **#27 Roasted Tomato Soup — 5 srv (soup only — GC fresh per meal)**
   - Roast tomatoes + yellow onions + bell pepper + garlic bulbs with olive oil + salt (425°F ~30 min)
   - Squeeze roasted garlic into blender + roasted veg + tomato paste + thyme + basil + black pepper + stock
   - Blend smooth (~60 sec), transfer to pot
   - Stir in 240mL nonfat milk, simmer 5 min on low (don't boil)
   - Portion 5 containers (soup base only)
   - **White-sauce swirl + grilled cheese assembled fresh per meal**
   - ~45 min
