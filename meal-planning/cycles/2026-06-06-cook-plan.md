0. **LUNCH ~12pm — #23 Burgers + Fries, 2 srv** *(Will + Elena, ~25 min total)*

1. **After lunch: start #18 slow cooker** *(unattended 2-3 hr)*
   - Slow cooker: 25 oz chicken breast + 1.5 Tbsp butter + 1.5 Tbsp dried minced onion + 0.75 Tbsp salt + 1.5 tsp garlic powder + 0.75 tsp black pepper
   - Foil packet (in slow cooker): 8 garlic cloves + 0.75 tsp oil + pinch salt
   - HIGH 2-3 hr OR LOW 3-4 hr until shreddable
   - ~5 min hands-on; oven stays free

2. **#24 Bowls — 14 srv** *(8 chicken chunks + 6 pork loin; slow cooker free → oven + air fryer parallel)*
   - **Air fryer:** Kirkland breaded chunks (400°F, 10-12 min from frozen — crispier than oven)
   - **Oven sheet pan A:** cubed pork loin tossed in avocado oil + garlic powder + paprika + cumin + S/P (425°F ~18-20 min, pull at 145°F internal, rest 5 min)
   - **Oven sheet pan B:** frozen veg medley + fresh broccoli + avocado oil + salt + pepper + garlic powder + paprika (425°F ~25 min)
   - Portion 14 containers (8 with breaded chunks, 6 with pork loin cubes — alternate by day for variety)
   - ~60 min total (vs 90 with all-oven workflow)

3. **#26 Salmon + Hummus filling prep — 3 srv** *(during oven downtime)*
   - Drain 3 cans wild salmon, flake into bowl
   - Mix with lemon juice + black pepper
   - Portion 3 sealed containers (~3 oz salmon mix each)
   - **Bread toasted fresh per meal; hummus pulled from tub per meal**
   - ~10 min

4. **#18 Alfredo finish — when slow cooker chicken is shreddable**
   - Shred chicken, reserve ALL slow cooker juices
   - Boil fettuccine HALF box time (very al dente), reserve 1/2 cup pasta water
   - Blend sauce in 2 batches: cottage cheese + cream cheese + parm + asiago + nonfat milk
   - Combine in large pot: pasta + chicken + juices + sauce + pasta water
   - Simmer LOW 15-20 min until pasta done + sauce thickens
   - Portion 8 containers
   - ~30 min hands-on after slow cooker finishes
