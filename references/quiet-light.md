# Quiet Light — Online Business Brokerage

Source: quietlight.com, podcast

---

## Core Thesis

Mid-market online business brokerage ($500k–$5M+ range). Higher quality and vetting than Flippa. Advisors are former entrepreneurs who have built and sold their own businesses. Podcast covers buyer-side education extensively.

---

## Why Relevant

Best-in-class brokerage for remote/online business acquisitions in Will's eventual price range. Good for SaaS, content, ecommerce, and professional services that operate remotely — fits Elena's absentee/remote preference.
